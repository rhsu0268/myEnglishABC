May 29, 2016

Welcome to myEnglishABC, an initiative to help people learn English. As we all know, when we travel to a country that we have never been to before, and do not speak the country's native language, we are often thrown into a state of fear and unease. We are afraid to walk into a restarunt, we are afraid to get on a taxi, and we may be even afraid to use the restroom.

The goal of myEnglishABC is to provide tools to help people navigate and learn English in easy and effective ways. We have an electronic dictionary functionality that allows you to look up any word or sentence and get a translation in a second. We have a way for you to save the words and sentences that you want to keep and also play it back through audio output. Finally, we have compiled a set of Follow Me videos from Great Britain to help you learn English painlessly.

Above all, we hope that this application, a work in progress will meet the challenges of some of the barriers in learning English and the most important thing is to have fun learning.

Cheers!
